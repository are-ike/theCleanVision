ABOUT US

The Clean Vision is a non-profit organization that is passionate about creating a clean and healthy environment where all living things can thrive and is determined to lend a hand to achieve the sustainanbility goals of the planet. We seek to provide quick and adequate services to aid and improve municipal plastic waste disposal and collection in the Bariga Local Council Development Area, provide awareness on waste management and empower the citizens through recycling.

OUR SERVICES

WASTE MANAGEMENT
We offer quick pick-up of plastic waste from households and organizations with efficient transport infrastructure, and ensure proper disposal.
RECYCLING
We recycle plastic through our partnership with various recycling companies as well as our recycling plants.
SUSTAINABILITY
We ensure sustainanbility of the environment by providing plastic-free water bodies and sanitation, and proserving life on land and in water.
