FAQs

Q: Will I Get Paid For Giving Away My Plastic Waste?

A: Yes, You Can Choose To Get Paid For The Plastic You Give To Us.

Q: Who Can Register On Your Website?

A: Everyone Is Welcome To Register On Our Website.

Q: How Can I Become A Volunteer?

A: You Can Become A Volunteer By Indicating So When Registering. If You Have Already Registered, You Can Contact Us Here.

Q: How Much Will I Get For The Plastic I Give?

A: Our Payment System Incorporates The Use Of Points. One Point Is Equivalent To One Naira. You Can Find Out More When You Sign Into Your Account.

Q: When Will I Get My Money?

A: You Can Redeem Your Money At The End Of Every Quarter.

Q: Will I Have To Deliver The Plastic Myself?

A: No, We Have Adequate Transport Facilities And Will Come To You.
